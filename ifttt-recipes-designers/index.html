IFTTT recipes for designers

By Benoît Boucart 26 Dec 2013

I discovered IFTTT (acronym for If This Than That) a month ago. If you don’t know this tool, it is an automation service for creating tasks between (external) services. To do so, you can create recipes (see further for examples).

© Picjumbo

Here are some recipes you can use:

Add a copy of your new Dribbble shot to...

Dribbble creates a RSS feed with the shots of a specific user. For example my RSS link with my latest Dribbble shots is: http://dribbble.com/benske/shots.rss (replace your username in this URL)

With IFTTT you can makes recipes from those RSS feeds.

Automatically post your new Dribbble shots on Twitter

For this I created an IFTTT recipe, with the following description:

If new feed item from http://dribbble.com/benske/shots.rss, then post a tweet to @benoitboucart

This recipe is public, so you can use & update it:

Other useful recipes...

This is just to show you what’s possible with IFTTT. You can also explore trending recipes or create your own, personalised ones.
